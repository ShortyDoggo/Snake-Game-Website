Why did we pick to do the game Snake?

Our first thought, when getting assigned this task was, what is a game that everyone seems to enjoy and likes to play when bored? Well, throught this whole school year our class has bonded over many things. One of them includong PC games. When a lection got cancelled, or when we were sitting in classrooms during winter, or even during the 5 minute breaks; a game that was most played throughout this whole year was Snake. So as a group we decided to recreate Snake and add our own touch to it. Still, I had never previously attempted something like this before. It was quite an enlightening experience.
